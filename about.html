The Beinomugisha Grace Foundation

This is a charitable organisation that was formed after the passing of Beinomugisha Grace to take on three major interventions, firstly, "Education", "Health" and "social Pyscho support". through out her life on earth these were strong pilors as to which she made critical sacrifies in the lives of many. Her children saw it imparative to take on her charitable deads as a way of keeping her legacy alive. through our own lives your legacy shall live on.
